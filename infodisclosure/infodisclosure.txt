Briefly explain the vulnerability and why the secure version prevents the vulnerability.
In insecure.js mongodb queries (SQL Injection) could be passed as input for username which can lead to exposure of data to mallicious user. 

In secure version we sanitize the input and then pass it to the mongoose query.
